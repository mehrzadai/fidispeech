Fidispeech, a Multi-Language Audiobook Generator, aims to generate audio from text, taking into account various context modalities. A critical component of the Fidispeech system is “Ravi”, a multi-language Persian text-to-speech system capable of style transfer and prosody control. Serving as the primary Text-to-Speech (TTS) module for the Fidibo product, Ravi will be accessible to users on a limited basis starting from January 2024.
